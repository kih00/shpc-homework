3. Model Background

Tensors

Data is primarily handled in units called tensors.

Dimensions range from 1D (Vector) to 4D (Conv filter).

Definitions are in include/tensor.h and implementations in src/tensor.cu.

RMSNorm

Normalizes the hidden state by its root mean square.

$$\text{RMSNorm}(x_{i}) = \frac{x_{i}}{\text{RMS}(x)} \cdot \gamma_{i} = \frac{x_{i}}{\sqrt{\frac{1}{D}\sum_{j=1}^{D}x_{j}^{2}+\epsilon}} \cdot \gamma_{i}$$

Rotary Positional Embedding (RoPE)

Encodes the absolute position of tokens by applying a rotation matrix to Query and Key vectors.

Logic:


$$q_{rotated} = q \cdot \cos + \text{rotate\_half}(q) \cdot \sin$$

Implementation logic:

First half: q1 * cos + (-q2) * sin

Second half: q2 * cos + q1 * sin

Convolution Block (ShortConv)

Projects input into three streams ($B, C, X$).

Applies a causal 1D convolution to the gated input ($B \times X_{gate}$) to capture local dependencies.

Multiplies the convolved output by the $C$ stream (gating) to produce the result.

Sparse Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) Block

Leverages a Router to selectively send each token's input to a small set of Top-4 SiLU Experts.

Computes the weighted sum of the experts' outputs.
